Programming A Number Library

Convert Number to Words

Minimum of zero and maximum of one million.

Output will be placed in the gray area once button is pressed.

Enter the number in the text field.

Convert Words to Number

Enter the words by using the dropdown. Starts from left to right.
For ten, twenty, thirty, etc., enter the ones digit then the tens.
For ten to nineteen, enter the tens first then the ones.

Convert Words to Currency

Pick the currency and enter the words by using the dropdown. Starts from left to right.

Number Delimited

Enter the number in the first text field, delimiter in the second, and the number of jumps where the delimiter will appear (starts from right to left).
